PRISTINE WALL — In a stunning turn of events that has sent shockwaves through the server, I'm writing this not just as your trusted reporter, but as the Marshal of the accidental victorious ★Hydra★ alliance.

Twenty-four hours ago, I was hiding in bushes taking notes. Today, I'm standing on the conquered Pristine Wall, still holding my notebook in one hand and a +11 Refined weapon in the other. The sight is surreal: ★Hydra★ banners flying where Antanta's once stood for weeks.

Let me be clear: I never wanted this. Territorial war? Too much responsibility. Politics? Please. I just saw an undefended wall and thought, "Huh, that'd be funny to take." Next thing I know, I'm standing on Pristine Wall—thanking Larura, my second in command, Warlord of ★Hydra★ for the victory. She did the actual work while I was taking notes from the backline.

"I was watching the whole thing," said Gaeto, standing nearby with a digital fire extinguisher just in case. "No one was actually defending. The war ended while I was still debating if I should deploy my celestial army to defend it or declare it non-legit. The problem? The war declaration was filed when Antanta still existed. They disbanded right before the war started. So it's not a PvP territorial war... but it's also not a PvE territorial war."

He paused, looking at the ★Hydra★ banners now flying over Pristine Wall.

"And then Outlaws did the exact same thing at Lunar Well. Same day. Same loophole. Two undefended territories, two alliances claiming victory while I'm standing here with my celestial army going '...do I deploy? Do I not?'"

He rubbed his temples.

"In the end, I can only say this: it's a perfectly abused, unforeseen opportunity. ★Hydra★ didn't break the rules. Outlaws didn't break the rules. They just... walked through a door that shouldn't have been open. I'm not sure if I should be impressed, update the rulebook, or both. Probably both."

The Accidental Conquest

How did a reporter's alliance end up with a territory with only 2 active personnel? One word: Luck.

"Eunice literally said 'LOL let's take Pristine Wall' in alliance chat," recalls a ★Hydra★ member who wished to remain anonymous. "I never thought she was serious. Next thing I know, I am standing on a conquered territory. I still don't know what happened."

The shocking disbandment of one of the Big Three alliances left every territory Antanta once controlled completely undefended—Pristine Wall, Lunar Well, and all the others, literally no one home. But according to Gaeto, those days are over: all former Antanta territories are now considered neutral ground, and the Celestial Army will be deployed for all future wars over them. No more undefended walls or wells! No more taking candies from babies!
